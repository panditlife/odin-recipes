Lemonade Recipe

Description:

Lemonade is a sweetened lemon-flavored beverage. There are varieties of lemonade found throughout the world. In North America and South Asia, cloudy lemonade dominates. It is traditionally a homemade drink using lemon juice, water, and a sweetener such as cane sugar, simple syrup or honey.

  • Prep: 10 minutes
  • Total: 10 minutes
  • Servings: 6
  • 6 lemons
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 6 cups water, or more as needed

Directions:

  1. Step 1

    2. Juice lemons; you should have 1 cup juice.

  2. Step 2

    3. Combine juice, sugar, and water in a 1/2-gallon pitcher. Stir until sugar dissolves. Taste and add more water if desired.

  3. Step 3

    4. Chill and serve over ice.

Nutrition Facts

Per serving: 151 calories; protein 1.3g; carbohydrates 44.9g; fat 0.3g; sodium 3.2mg.
