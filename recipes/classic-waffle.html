Waffle Recipe

Description:

A waffle is a dish made from leavened batter or dough that is cooked between two plates that are patterned to give a characteristic size, shape, and surface impression. There are many variations based on the type of waffle iron and recipe used.

  • Prep: 10 minutes
  • Cook: 15 minutes
  • Total: 25 minutes
  • Servings: 5
  • Yield: 10 to 12 waffles
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:

  1. Step 1

    2. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.

  2. Step 2

    3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.

  3. Step 3

    4. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
